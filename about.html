About Just For Phishing

Building a safer digital world through interactive cybersecurity education

🎣 About Us

Just For Phishing is a free, open-source cybersecurity training platform designed to help individuals and organizations recognize and defend against phishing attacks, social engineering, and other common cyber threats.

We believe that cybersecurity education should be accessible, engaging, and practical. Through interactive simulations, realistic scenarios, and hands-on learning experiences, we empower users to develop the skills and awareness needed to stay safe online.

Our platform was born from a simple observation: traditional security awareness training is often boring, forgettable, and disconnected from real-world threats. We set out to change that by creating immersive, scenario-based training that feels more like a game than a lecture.

🎯 Our Mission

Our mission is to make cybersecurity awareness training accessible, engaging, and effective for everyone—from individuals protecting their personal accounts to organizations safeguarding sensitive data.

We aim to reduce the success rate of phishing attacks by equipping people with the knowledge and muscle memory to identify threats instinctively. Through realistic simulations and immediate feedback, we help users build lasting security habits.
